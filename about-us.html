About Us

Professional Aluminum Packaging Manufacturer in China

Company Profile

Radiant Packaging is a professional manufacturer dedicated to aluminum packaging products and related components. Our product range covers aerosol cans, threaded bottles, beverage aluminum bottles, two-piece valves, and complete plastic accessories.

All products can be fully customized in terms of shape, size, and design, helping our clients enhance their brand value and market competitiveness.

Our Manufacturing System

We operate three specialized production facilities:

  • 🏭 Fully automated aluminum aerosol can factory
  • 🏭 Injection molding factory for plastic components
  • 🏭 Precision valve manufacturing factory

Engineering & Technical Capabilities

With strong technical expertise and deep industry experience, we provide full-process support including:

  • ✔ Product R&D and structural design
  • ✔ Mold design and manufacturing
  • ✔ Injection molding and precision forming
  • ✔ Automated assembly systems
  • ✔ Filling technology integration
  • ✔ Packaging solution consulting

Our Value

We provide flexible and eco-friendly packaging solutions for businesses of all sizes. By combining sustainable materials with high-quality manufacturing, we help our clients improve product appeal and market performance.

Our mission is to deliver reliable, innovative, and sustainable aluminum packaging solutions to global customers.
